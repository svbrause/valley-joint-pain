Getting here

By car, transit, or rideshare.

Whatever way you're coming, Van Nuys is well-connected.

By car

Free parking is available in our on-site lot. We're just off the 405 at the Hamlin St exit, or easily reached via Sepulveda Blvd. The parking lot has accessible spaces close to the entrance.

By bus or Metro

Multiple DASH and Metro bus routes serve Sepulveda Blvd through Van Nuys. The Van Nuys Orange Line station (G Line BRT) is approximately 1 mile away and connects across the Valley.

By rideshare

Uber and Lyft both serve Van Nuys reliably. If you're having a procedure that day (and will need a driver home), rideshare drop-off is right at the main entrance. Let us know when you book.

Patients we see

We serve patients from across Los Angeles.

While we're based in Van Nuys, we routinely see patients from throughout the San Fernando Valley and greater Los Angeles. Many patients travel from West LA, South Bay, and the Conejo Valley specifically for procedures that aren't widely available at local practices.
